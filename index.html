About me

I am a graduate student and researcher at the University of North Carolina - Chapel Hill, pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science. I am currently working with Dr. Neil Gaikwad on computer vision projects. Previously, I contributed to research in the High-Performance Computing (HPC) Lab under Dr. Dong Dai and the Machine Learning (ML) Lab under Dr. Srijan Das at UNC Charlotte. My work spans leveraging artificial intelligence for synthetic dataset generation, job trace analysis in computing clusters, and temporal action detection in videos. I have co-authored papers published in JSSPP'24 and IPDPS'24, with my research featured at top-tier conferences. My work is fueled by an unwavering passion for creating impactful solutions and building innovative applications that bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications.
